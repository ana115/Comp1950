Strategy Solutions

We'll be using a combination of HTML5, CSS3 and JQuery to allow students to find the resources to succeed easily

Digital Design

The main purpose of the design is making it easy for students to find the material during class and afterwards be able to easily go back to notes, exercises and homeworks if they wish to review them. This is why will be changing the layout of the lecture pages by adding homeworks and exercises tabs, as well as an easy navigation of the content of the lecture.

SEO

Even though SEO is not a priority for the website since it target audience is very specifict but we'll still be keeping SEO best practices in mind. We'll make sure the website is fast, effcient and that the content is relevant to the topic

Graphic Design

We put a lot of effort in design, as it is the most important ingredient of successful website. We'll be going for a neutral background in gray and then we'll add a pop of color with different color banners.

Analytics

We'll be adding Google Analytics to the website so you can have more information about how much time the student are spending in each section and how they are using the resources

Dedicated Support

We'll be providing continuous support and be available for any functionality questions that could arise.
